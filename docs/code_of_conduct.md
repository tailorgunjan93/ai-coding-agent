# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Overview
This project is committed to fostering an open, welcoming, and respectful community.  
All contributors, maintainers, and community members are expected to uphold these standards.

---

## Our Standards
Contributors are expected to:
- Be respectful and inclusive in all interactions.
- Provide constructive feedback and engage in healthy debate.
- Document decisions transparently in issues, PRs, or `planning.md`.
- Treat testing, security, and design enforcement as non-negotiable quality gates.
- Avoid discriminatory, offensive, or harassing behavior.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior
Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Discrimination based on race, gender, sexual orientation, religion, or disability.
- Harassment in public or private communications.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Ignoring project governance, security, or testing rules.

---

## Responsibilities
- **Maintainers**: Enforce the Code of Conduct, review reports, and take corrective action.
- **Contributors**: Follow the standards, report violations, and support a positive environment.
- **Community Members**: Encourage respectful collaboration and raise concerns when necessary.

---

## Enforcement
- Violations may result in warnings, PR rejections, or removal from the project.
- Severe or repeated violations may result in permanent bans.
- Reports should be submitted via project maintainers or issue tracker.
